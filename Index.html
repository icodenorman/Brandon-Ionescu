Kaido

Kaido is my mythical creature. He is a beast that was once a human without his devil fruit, "Dragon."

Over the past years, the being has reeked havoc over the islands of sky, underwater world, and his homeland town.

People like to call him a beast. He is basically invincible due to his devil fruit that I mentioned earlier.
